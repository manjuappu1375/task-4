The Brave Little Mouse
Summary: In a cozy little house, a tiny mouse named Max dreams of being brave like 
the lions he sees in books. One day, when a big cat comes to the house, 
Max gathers his courage and distracts the cat by leading it on a chase. 
The other animals cheer for Max, proving that bravery comes in all sizes!



 The Kindness Tree
Summary: In a village, there stands a magical tree that grows colorful fruits 
every time someone does a good deed. When the villagers realize this, 
they begin helping each other, and the tree blossoms with amazing colors. 
They learn that kindness not only helps others but also brings joy to themselves
